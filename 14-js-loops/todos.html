JavaScript To-do List

Hello and welcome to your new favorite lightweight to-do list! All commands and feedback are managed through the JavaScript console, so be sure to open up your developer tools to proceed.

Available Commands:

  • "list" or "l" - Lists all of your to-dos
  • "add" or "a" - Adds an item to your to-do list
  • "remove" or "r" - Removes an item from your to-do list
  • "quit" or "q" - Quits the application
  • "clear" or "c" - Clears the console log
